Ducati Superleggera V4

The second-place bike on this ranking of the fastest motorcycles in the world is the Ducati Superleggera V4. The whole load-bearing chassis of the Superleggera V4 is composed entirely of carbon fibre, making it the only street-legal motorcycle. It is a work of art. Currently the most sophisticated litre-class supersport bike available, it is fully equipped with top-notch machinery. A liquid-cooled, 998cc, V4 cylinder, DOHC, 16 valve engine powers the Ducati Superleggera V4. With a full race exhaust system, it generates a maximum of 234 HP and a maximum torque of 119 Nm. It boasts a maximum speed of more than over 300 km/h and a power-to-weight ratio of 1.30 hp/kg.
